# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 27, 2026**

Habit Tracker ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information within the mobile application.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

To provide a seamless experience, the App processes the following types of data:

- **User-Generated Content**: Information regarding your habits, reminders, and financial transactions (income/expenses) that you enter manually. This data is stored locally on your device.
- **Backup and Sync (Optional)**: If you use the manual cloud backup feature, your email address is used solely for identifying your account and synchronizing your data.

## 2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

This App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data to show relevant ads to you. This information may include:

- Advertising IDs (e.g., Android Advertising ID)
- Device information and basic usage statistics
- Cookies for ad frequency capping and reporting

For more details, please refer to: [Google Advertising Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)

## 3. Pro Features and Data Export

The App provides advanced features such as:

- **Advanced Analytics**: Visual charts and progress statistics based on your local habit data
- **Data Export**: You have the right to export your data in PDF or CSV formats at any time
- **Manual Backups**: All data remains under your control and can be restored from your personal backup files

## 4. Data Security

We value your trust. Your financial and personal habit data is stored locally on your device by default. We do not use third-party backend databases (like Firebase) at this stage. We recommend using the manual backup feature to prevent data loss.

## 5. Third-Party Links and Services

The App includes links to third-party services:

- Google Play Services
- Google AdMob

## 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as we add new features (such as real-time database synchronization). You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
